# Smart-AI-Employee-Performance-Insights-Dashboard

Power BI dashboard analyzing employee performance using AI based logic, DAX measures, and HR focused KPIs to support data driven decision making.

# Key Features
1) AI based high performance identification.
2) Attrition risk analysis using business logic.
3) Departments and role wise performance insights.
4) Overtime impact on performance.
5) Performance Rate Years of Experience.

# Insights
1) The overall attrition is around 15% indicating a fairly stable workforce.
2) Only 11% of employees fall into the high performer category, highlighting scope for performance improvement.
3) Performance levels vary clearly across departments, showing role and team based impact.
4) Employees working overtime show slightly higher average performance compared to non-overtime employees.
5) Higher job satisfaction is associated with lower attrition rates.
6) Most employees do not work overtime,yet the overtime group demonstrates marginally better performance.

# Tools:
Power BI, DAX, AI Features, KPI Cards.
